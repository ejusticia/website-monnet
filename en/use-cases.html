Recurring collections · Low risk

Basic services & subscriptions

Telecom, utilities, streaming and SaaS need to collect recurring payments across the region — with methods customers already use and minimal friction.

With Monnet you collect via direct debit and local methods (PIX, SPEI, PSE, bank transfers) in a single flow. One integration covers multiple countries; no need to open local accounts or set up entities in each market. Compliance and reconciliation are handled, so you can focus on retention and product.

  • One API for recurring collections in every LATAM market we serve
  • Local payment methods and bank debit — less friction, higher conversion
  • Local compliance and automated reconciliation; fewer failed payments and disputes

Payins · Refunds · Multicurrency

E-Commerce & Retail

Stores and marketplaces need to accept how each country pays — card, wallets, instant transfer — and handle returns and reconciliation without building operations in every market.

We offer local payment methods in a single integration: PIX, SPEI, PSE, cards and more. Shoppers pay with what they already have; you get real-time settlement and full visibility. Refunds at scale and multi-currency reconciliation without opening local bank accounts or dealing with multiple providers.

  • Checkout with local methods in every country we serve
  • Centralized refunds and reconciliation; less manual work
  • One API for collection; same integration experience in every market

Real-time payouts · Mass disbursement

Mobility & Gig Platforms

Drivers, couriers and freelancers expect to get paid when the job ends — in their currency, without waiting days. Platforms need to disburse to thousands of people reliably and with full traceability.

Monnet lets you pay fleets and gig workers in real time when their shift or task ends. One API for mass disbursements across multiple countries; settlement in hours, not days. Broad availability and local currency in each market, so retention doesn’t depend on payment speed.

  • Real-time payouts and mass disbursements with one integration
  • Local currency and availability that scales with your operation
  • Traceability and reconciliation for compliance and support

Cross-border · FX · B2B

Travel & Hospitality

Agencies, OTAs and operators collect in the traveller’s country and pay hotels, transport and local suppliers. FX exposure and multi-country liquidity are critical.

With our API you collect in any country we serve and pay local suppliers instantly. Manage FX exposure and B2B settlement from a single dashboard, without setting up local structures in each destination. Visibility into flows by market and currency for better treasury decisions.

  • Collect in the customer’s country and pay local suppliers with one API
  • FX and B2B settlement management with real-time visibility
  • Fewer intermediaries and less regulatory friction per country

Banking rails · Expansion

Neobanks & Fintechs

Launching in a new country usually means licenses, bank agreements and months of integration. Fintechs that want to grow in LATAM need an infrastructure layer that’s already there.

Monnet acts as your regional payments layer: one API, clear documentation and direct connection to local rails (PIX, SPEI, PSE, etc.). You can launch in new markets in weeks, not months. We handle compliance and network operations; you focus on product, UX and growth.

  • One integration for multiple countries; add markets without new contracts
  • Direct banking rails and enterprise-grade security and availability
  • Documentation and support built for product and engineering teams
