About Monnet

The payment partner that LATAM’s leading brands trust. We build the same kind of unified, API-first payment layer that companies like Stripe, Adyen, and Checkout.com built for mature markets — but purpose-built for Latin America’s rails, regulations, and growth.

Reliable infrastructure.
Enterprise-grade from day one.

Global paytechs set the bar: one API, clear docs, and compliance you can rely on. We apply that playbook to LATAM — direct banking rails, AWS-backed infrastructure, and ISO 27001 and PCI DSS compliance — so you don’t have to stitch together local acquirers and legacy providers.

◇

Coverage that scales

9 countries, one API. Like Rapyd or Adyen in other regions, we give you direct connections to local rails — PIX, SPEI, PSE, and more — without intermediaries. Real-time settlement and full visibility across markets.

◇

Partners you trust

Built on AWS, the same backbone that powers Stripe, Netflix, and enterprise workloads worldwide. We rely on the same infrastructure and security standards so you get reliability and scale from day one.

◇

Compliance first

ISO 27001 and PCI DSS compliant — the same certifications that matter to Adyen, Checkout.com, and global processors. Your data and your customers’ payments are protected by design, so you can sell to regulated industries and enterprise buyers.

Our Story

Born in LATAM.
Built for LATAM.

In the US and Europe, Stripe, Adyen, and Checkout.com replaced fragmented payment stacks with a single, developer-first platform. LATAM never had that — until now.

We were founded by payments veterans who lived the pain: direct-to-bank technology, profitability from day one, and the acquisition of ETpay — Chile’s leading instant payment fintech — to own the rails, not just integrate them. Today we operate in 9 countries with 300+ Monnetiers, and we’re backed by recognition from MIT Technology Review, Forbes, and top accelerators.
